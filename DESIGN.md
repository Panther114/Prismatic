# PRISMATIC Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

PRISMATIC is a dark, cinematic studio instrument: graphite software chrome surrounds an open spectral canvas where the music becomes a mirrored horizon of frequency pillars and flowing perspective ribbons. A rotating vinyl record with the track's real embedded cover is the focal player in both the app and exported frame. The interface stays restrained and precise so the color belongs to the sound, not to decoration.

## Colors

- Canvas black: `#05070c`
- Graphite surface: `#0b0f17`
- Ivory text: `#f3f0e8`
- Muted steel: `#8e96a5`
- Spectral accents: `#13b8ff`, `#7047ff`, `#f12cbf`, `#ff6a32`, `#ffd44d`

## Typography

- Display voice: Barlow Condensed, 700–800
- Interface voice: IBM Plex Sans, 350–600
- Timing/data voice: JetBrains Mono, 400–600 with tabular numerals

## Motion

- Bass expands the low-frequency architecture and depth glow.
- Midrange moves the ribbon terrain; treble sharpens the fine pillars and sparks.
- UI transitions are brief and controlled; the exported visual breathes continuously with the track.

## What NOT to Do

- No generic rainbow cycling, strobing, or color detached from frequency position.
- No glassmorphism stacks, floating orbs, neon grids, or rounded-card dashboard clutter.
- No tiny metadata or web-scale typography in the rendered video.
- No nondeterministic animation; every render must be seekable and reproducible.
